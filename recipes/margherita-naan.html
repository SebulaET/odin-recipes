Margherita Naan

Margherita Pizza but it's Naan

I love basil. I also love cheese. You know what else I like? Bread. Thus, I present you, Margherita Naan.

This recipe will not be very involved, you only need to chop up a couple things and turn on the oven, so go ahead and get an oven mitt and a sharp knife ready. We will also be using pesto instead of marinara or some other pizza sauce but you can use whatever you like in place of that!

Ingredients

  • Naan bread, about four pieces
  • One large tomato, beefsteak or other similar variety
  • One big ol' ball of Mozzarella
  • A bunch of basil

Steps

  1. Wash everything!
  2. Slice up your tomato into one inch pieces or quartered into wedges if you have extra, might be prettier.
  3. Slice the mozzarella into little discs, no choice here.
  4. Spread your pesto, or other sauce, onto the naan. Nice.
  5. Stack everything however looks most appetizing on top of your saucy naan.
  6. Broil on low in about five minute increments 'til the cheese looks like a nice toasty marshmallow.
  7. Top with olive oil and some S&P.
